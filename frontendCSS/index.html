Technical Domains

Pick a track, build momentum, then explore beyond it.

We organize our activities around four core domains. Each has dedicated mentors, resources, and regular sessions.

Coding & DSA

Problem-solving sessions, contest prep, peer practice, and the confidence to think clearly under pressure.

Web Development

Frontend polish, backend APIs, deployment pipelines, and real projects that look and feel production-ready.

AI & Machine Learning

Models, data thinking, notebooks, prototypes, and practical ways to apply machine intelligence.

Design & UI/UX

Visual systems, product thinking, interfaces, and experiences people actually enjoy using.

What We Do

More than a club — a launchpad for your career.

Hands-On Workshops

We run regular workshops on web dev, Git, Python, cloud — always with live coding, not just slides.

Hackathons & Contests

Internal code sprints, design challenges, and team hackathons to build under real constraints.

Peer Mentorship

Seniors guide juniors through projects, interview prep, and the rough patches of learning something new.

Tech Talks

Students and guest speakers share real-world experiences — from startup life to research breakthroughs.

Project Showcases

Ship your project, present it, get feedback, iterate. Build a portfolio before you graduate.

Global ACM Network

Part of the world's largest computing society — access events, resources, and a global community.

Ready to start building?

Whether you're writing your first line of code or shipping your tenth project — there's a place for you here.
